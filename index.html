About me

Everything in our life does not happen by chance. So at one point I began to be interested in html and css, completely unaware that I would like to link my life with it. And I just wanted to help my friends refine their site.

Over time, I sucked. During the day I worked as an accountant, and in the evening I went to courses for front-end developers, wrote a code at the weekend. Over time, I began to order sites. And I realized that it was time to turn a hobby into a profession.

I want to be part of a big team, to contribute to the development of a large project. I want to develop and eventually become the leader of such a project.
